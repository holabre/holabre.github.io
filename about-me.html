About Me

Hi! My name is Quoc. I am from East Side San Jose, California and was raised in a small ethnic enclave known as Little Saigon. Throughout my early academic years, I heavily struggled learning the topics in schools which resulted in me being constantly bullied. I struggled to do well in school both socially and academically. However, I still progressed as a student and a person. After going to middle school at Alpha: Blanca Alvarado Middle School, I eventually became a better student in where I was my class's Valedictorian. More importantly, during this time I learned about the community that I was from and the struggles we all had. Rather if it was from lack of resources, gang violence, and etc, I started to realize and understand more. Consequentially, it influenced me to give back to my community in any way that I saw possible. In high school, I was volunteering for any community events there was as well as tutoring at a local middle school. I learned a lot from being around my community and the experiences I had and am truly blessed for all the events that has happened in my life. I knew that I wanted to futher represent my community and my city, and I knew the best way to do so was going to the best university. Currently, I am now studying at UC Berkeley in where I continue to learn more about myself and others while also representing my community and showing that despite circumstances, we can do anything we put our minds into it.
